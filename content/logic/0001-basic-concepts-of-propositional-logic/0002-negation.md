# Negation

Given the statement $p$ and $q$, we define **the negation** (or **denial**) of $p$, denoted $\neg p$ and read **not p**, is true precisely when $p$ is false.


| $p$   | $\neg p$  |
|-------|-----------|
| T     | F         |
| F     | T         |
