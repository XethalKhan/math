# Disjunction

The **disjunction** (or **alteration**) of $p$ and $q$, denoted $p \lor q$ and read **p or q**, is true when one or the other or both of the statements $p$ and $q$ is (or are) true.

| $p$   | $q$   | $p \lor q$    |
|-------|-------|---------------|
| T     | T     | T             |
| T     | F     | T             |
| F     | T     | T             |
| F     | F     | F             |
