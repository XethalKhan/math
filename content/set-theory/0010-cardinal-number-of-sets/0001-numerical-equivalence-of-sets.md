# Numerical equivalence of sets

Let $A$ and $B$ be **sets**. We say that $A$ and $B$ are **numerically equivalent**, denoted $A \cong B$, if and only if there exists a **one-to-one** mapping $f: A \rightarrow B$ of $A$ onto $B$.
