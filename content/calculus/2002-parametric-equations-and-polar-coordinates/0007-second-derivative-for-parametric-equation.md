# Second derivative for parametric equation

$$
\frac{d^{2}y}{dx^{2}} = \frac{d}{dx}(\frac{dy}{dx}) = \frac{\frac{d}{dt}(\frac{dy}{dx})}{\frac{dx}{dt}}
$$

It is important to note that,

$$
\frac{d^{2}y}{dx^{2}} \neq \frac{\frac{d^{2}y}{dt^{2}}}{\frac{d^{2}x}{dt^{2}}}
$$
