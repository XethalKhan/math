# Trigonometry functions related to each other

$$
cos(x)
\\;\\;\\;\\;\\;
\\;\\;\\;\\;\\;
\\;\\;\\;\\;\\;
\\;\\;\\;\\;\\;
\\;\\;\\;\\;\\;
sin(x)
$$

$$
tan(x) = \frac{sin(x)}{cos(x)}
\\;\\;\\;\\;\\;
\\;\\;\\;\\;\\;
\\;\\;\\;\\;\\;
\\;\\;\\;\\;\\;
cot(x) = \frac{cos(x)}{sin(x)} = \frac{1}{tan(x)}
$$

$$
sec(x) = \frac{1}{cos(x)}
\\;\\;\\;\\;\\;
\\;\\;\\;\\;\\;
\\;\\;\\;\\;\\;
\\;\\;\\;\\;\\;
\\;\\;\\;
csc(x) = \frac{1}{sin(x)}
$$
