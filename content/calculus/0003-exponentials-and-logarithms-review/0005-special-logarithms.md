# Special logarithms

There are a couple of special logarithms that arise in many places. These are,

$$
ln(x) = log_{e}(x)
\\;\\;\\;\\;\\;
\text{ This log is called the natural logarithm}
$$

$$
log(x) = log_{10}(x)
\\;\\;\\;\\;\\;
\text{ This log is called the common logarithm}
$$
