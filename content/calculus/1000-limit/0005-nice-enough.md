# Nice enough

Provided $f(x)$ is “nice enough” we have,

$$
\lim\limits_{x \rightarrow a} f(x) = f(a)
$$

$$
\lim\limits_{x \rightarrow a^{-}} f(x) = f(a)
$$

$$
\lim\limits_{x \rightarrow a^{+}} f(x) = f(a)
$$
