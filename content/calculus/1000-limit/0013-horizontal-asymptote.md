# Horizontal asymptote

The function $f(x)$ will have a **horizontal asymptote** at $y = L$ if either of the following are true.

$$
\lim\limits_{x \rightarrow \infty} f(x) = L
\\;\\;\\;
\lim\limits_{x \rightarrow - \infty} f(x) = L
$$
