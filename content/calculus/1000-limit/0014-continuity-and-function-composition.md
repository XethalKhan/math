# Continuity and function composition

If $f(x)$ is **continuous** at $x = b$ and $\lim\limits_{x \rightarrow a} g(x) = b$ then,

$$
\lim\limits_{x \rightarrow a} f(g(x)) = f(\lim\limits_{x \rightarrow a} g(x))
$$
