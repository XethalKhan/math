# Slope of a tangent line

Next to **rate of change**, this is the next major interpretation of the derivative. The **slope of the tangent line** to $f(x)$ at $x = a$ is $f'(a)$. The tangent line then is given by,

$$
y = f(a) + f'(a)(x - a)
$$
