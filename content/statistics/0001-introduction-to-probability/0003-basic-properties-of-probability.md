# Basic properties of probability

One can derive many properties of $\mathbb{P}$ from the axioms, such as:

$$
\mathbb{P}(\emptyset)=0
$$

$$
A \subset B \rightarrow \mathbb{P}(A) \leq \mathbb{P}(B)
$$

$$
0 \leq P(A) \leq 1
$$

$$
