# Complex conjugate of column vector

Suppose that $u$ is a vector from $\mathbb{C}^m$. Then the conjugate of the vector, $\overline{u}$, is defined by

$$
[\overline{u}]\_{i} = \overline{[u]\_{i}}
\\;\\;\\;
1 \leq i \leq m
$$
