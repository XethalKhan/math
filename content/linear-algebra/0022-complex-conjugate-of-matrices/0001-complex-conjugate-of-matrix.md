# Complex conjugate of a matrix

Suppose $A$ is an $m \times n$ matrix. Then the conjugate of $A$, written $\overline{A}$ is an $m \times n$ matrix defined by

$$
[\overline{A}]\_{ij} = \overline{[A]\_{ij}}
$$
