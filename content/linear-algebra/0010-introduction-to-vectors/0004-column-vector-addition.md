# Column vector addition

Suppose that $u, v \in \mathbb{C}^m$. The sum of $u$ and $v$ is the vector $u + v$ defined by

$$
[u + v]_i = [u]_i + [v]_i
\\;\\;\\;
\text{ for }
\\;\\;\\;
1 \leq i \leq m
$$

## Example

If

$$
u =
\begin{bmatrix}
    2\\
    -3\\
    4\\
    2
\end{bmatrix}
\\;\\;\\;
v =
\begin{bmatrix}
    -1\\
    5\\
    2\\
    -7
\end{bmatrix}
$$

$$
u + v
=
\begin{bmatrix}
    2\\
    -3\\
    4\\
    2
\end{bmatrix}
+
\begin{bmatrix}
    -1\\
    5\\
    2\\
    -7
\end{bmatrix}
=
\begin{bmatrix}
    2 + (-1)\\
    -3 + 5\\
    4 + 2\\
    2 + (-7)
\end{bmatrix}
=
\begin{bmatrix}
    1\\
    2\\
    6\\
    -5
\end{bmatrix}
$$
