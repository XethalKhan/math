# Determinant of matrices of size two

Suppose that $A = \begin{bmatrix}
    a & b\\
    c & d
\end{bmatrix}$. Then $det(A) = a d - b c$.

## Proof

Applying **Definition Determinant of a Matrix**,

$$
\begin{vmatrix}
    a & b\\
    c & d
\end{vmatrix}
= a | d | - b | c | = a d - b c
$$
